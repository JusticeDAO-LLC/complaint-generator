How To Use This Step

What to focus on in the interview

  • Describe the core event sequence in the order it happened.
  • Name the people, employer, agency, or entity involved.
  • Flag any documents, messages, screenshots, or witnesses that support key points.
  • Explain the harm suffered and any relief that may be requested later.
